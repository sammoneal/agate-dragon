Eileen Malone: Agate Dragon

Eileen Malone is a Washington based artist with decades of experience. She holds a degree in Art History and works in various media including watercolor, oil paint, pastel, pencil, and sumi-e. The name Agate Dragon is derived from the kanji representation of the word "Malone." Her sumi-e art ranges from tradional black ink to experimental works in a variety of colors. Each piece uses a minimal number of strokes to represent the subject's form, demeanor, and action.
